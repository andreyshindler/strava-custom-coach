# Training Zones Reference

## Power Zones (% of FTP)

| Zone | Name             | % FTP     | Feel                        | Typical Duration  |
|------|------------------|-----------|-----------------------------|-------------------|
| Z1   | Active Recovery  | < 55%     | Very easy, no effort        | Unlimited         |
| Z2   | Endurance        | 55–75%    | Comfortable, conversational | 1–6+ hours        |
| Z3   | Tempo            | 75–90%    | Moderately hard, focused    | 20 min – 2 hours  |
| Z4   | Threshold        | 90–105%   | Hard, sustainable ~1 hour   | 8–60 min          |
| Z5   | VO2 Max          | 105–120%  | Very hard, breathing hard   | 3–8 min           |
| Z6   | Anaerobic        | 120–150%  | Near max, short efforts     | 30 sec – 3 min    |
| Z7   | Neuromuscular    | > 150%    | Sprint, all-out             | < 30 sec          |

## Heart Rate Zones (% of max HR)

| Zone | Name         | % Max HR  |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| Z1   | Recovery     | < 68%     |
| Z2   | Aerobic base | 68–83%    |
| Z3   | Tempo        | 83–94%    |
| Z4   | Threshold    | 94–105%   |
| Z5   | Anaerobic    | > 105%    |

## Finding Your FTP

**Ramp Test (easiest):**
Start at 100W. Increase by 20W every minute until failure.
FTP ≈ 75% of max 1-minute power achieved.

**20-Minute Test:**
After warm-up, ride all-out for 20 minutes.
FTP ≈ 95% of average power over those 20 minutes.

**60-Minute Test (most accurate):**
Average power over a maximal 60-minute effort = FTP.

## TSS (Training Stress Score)

TSS = (duration_sec × NP × IF) / (FTP × 3600) × 100

Where:
- NP = Normalized Power
- IF = Intensity Factor (NP / FTP)

**Rule of thumb TSS per hour by zone:**
- Z1: ~30 TSS/hr
- Z2: ~45–55 TSS/hr
- Z3: ~65–75 TSS/hr
- Z4: ~85–95 TSS/hr
- Z5: ~100–110 TSS/hr

## Weekly TSS Guidelines

| Rider Level  | Weekly TSS (base) | Weekly TSS (peak) |
|--------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Beginner     | 150–250           | 300–400           |
| Intermediate | 300–450           | 500–650           |
| Advanced     | 500–700           | 800–1000+         |

Increase weekly TSS by no more than 10% per week to avoid overtraining.
